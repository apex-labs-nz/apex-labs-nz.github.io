Flagship · In development

SailNav

A passage-planning and navigation app for cruising sailors, born on a catamaran in the Marlborough Sounds. SailNav plans the passage you'd actually sail — not the one a fragile forecast optimum suggests.

Passage planning

Plans you can trust at sea

Weather-routed passage and departure planning built on your boat's real polars, with conservative speed models and honest "no good window" answers when that's the truth.

Tides & streams

Tidal gates, done properly

Authoritative tidal stream data for passages where timing is everything — including Cook Strait, Tory Channel, and French Pass — coupled directly to the passage timeline.

Live instruments

Your boat's data, everywhere

NMEA 0183 and Signal K integration brings wind, speed, depth, and AIS from your instruments to iPad, iPhone, and Apple TV — at the nav station, in the cockpit, or in the saloon.

Offline-first

Built for where there's no signal

Everything essential works without a connection. Forecasts, charts overlays, and planning data are cached aboard; the internet is a convenience, never a dependency.

The studio

About Apex Labs

Apex Labs Limited is an independent software company registered in New Zealand. We design and build software for iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV, with a focus on marine navigation and tools for the outdoors.

Our work is grounded in real use: SailNav's design decisions are made underway, on passage, in the conditions the software is meant to serve. We favour measured data over tuned constants, single sources of truth over duplication, and silence over false precision.
